## 1. Important Notice

EmoCare is intended for individuals **18 years of age or older**.

EmoCare is **not a medical device**, healthcare provider, therapist, counselor, crisis service, emergency service, or diagnostic tool.

EmoCare does not provide medical advice, mental health treatment, psychiatric services, crisis intervention, or emergency assistance.

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, self-harm thoughts, or believe you may be in danger, contact emergency services, a licensed healthcare provider, or a crisis hotline immediately.

## 4. AI Processing Providers

To provide EmoCare's features, certain information may be processed by trusted third-party service providers.

### Anthropic

EmoCare uses Anthropic's AI models to generate conversations and responses.

Information submitted through chats may be transmitted securely to Anthropic for processing.

Anthropic is contractually restricted from using customer data for model training when accessed through applicable commercial APIs.

### ElevenLabs

If voice functionality is enabled, audio may be processed through ElevenLabs to provide voice synthesis and voice interaction features.

### Tavily

EmoCare's Oracle and knowledge-search features may use Tavily to retrieve publicly available information from the internet.

Search requests may be transmitted to Tavily when Oracle functionality is used.
